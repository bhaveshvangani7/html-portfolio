My Top 3 movies of All-time

1. Interstellar

Interstellar is a Sci-fi movie which depicts space travel and more specifically speaking black hole and worm-hole. A retired astronaut is given chance to go back into space when the life on earth cannot be sustained for much further. he has to search for alternate worlds where life can thrive and human species can be saved from extinction

2. End Game

End game is also a Sci-fi movie based on the comic books of Marvel. This movie features all the MCU characters al-together fighting against Thanos-The Mad Titan where they un-do the previous snap done by Thanos in the movie Infinity War and also wipe out Thanos and his Army from the face of this Universe.

3. Inception

Inception is another Sci-fi movie made by the legend Christopher Nolan. The movie speaks about injecting an idea into a persons brain while he's asleep which appears as a dream but later becomes the life's reality of that person and the person continues to take decisions based on that dream.
